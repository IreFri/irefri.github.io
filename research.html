Research

Research themes
  • Humanoid locomotion — contact uncertainty, push recovery, uneven terrain
  • Compliant mechanisms — soft feet, compliant leg joints, impact handling
  • Variable impedance — adaptive compliance, safe physical interaction
  • Simulation-driven development — large-scale evaluation, Isaac Gym, MuJoCo
  • Learning-based analysis — stress testing, robustness optimization, RL
