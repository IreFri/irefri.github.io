Research

Research focus
  • Humanoid locomotion and stability analysis under contact-rich interaction
  • Compliant mechanisms, including soft feet and compliant leg joints
  • Variable impedance and adaptive compliance for robust physical interaction
  • Simulation-driven development using Isaac Gym and MuJoCo
  • Learning-based analysis for robustness and impact handling
