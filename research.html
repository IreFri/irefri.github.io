Research

Research focus
  • Humanoid locomotion and stability under contact-rich interaction
  • Compliant mechanisms (soft feet and leg joints) and variable impedance control
  • Simulation-driven development using Isaac Gym and MuJoCo
  • Learning-based methods for locomotion analysis and robustness evaluation
