Irene Frizza

Research Engineer developing robust locomotion and control systems for humanoid robots

I am a Robotics Research Engineer specializing in humanoid locomotion, contact-rich control, and adaptive compliance.

My work focuses on designing robust control and locomotion systems using physics-based simulation (Isaac Gym, MuJoCo) and reinforcement learning as an analysis and optimization tool.

I have hands-on experience with compliant actuation, soft feet and soft joints, and end-to-end pipelines spanning simulation, control design, and experimental robotics.
