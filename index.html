Irene Frizza

Research Engineer developing robust locomotion and control systems for humanoid robots

I design and study locomotion and control systems for humanoid robots, with a focus on robustness, physical interaction, and contact-rich behaviors. My work combines physics-based simulation, control design, and reinforcement learning to analyze and improve stability, impact handling, and adaptability on uneven terrain.

I have experience building full simulation-to-control pipelines and working across software, control, and hardware-oriented robotics projects.
